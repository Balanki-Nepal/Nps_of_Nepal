About us

With the goal of Nature Conservation 20 protected areas are established by the Government of Nepal. They cover landscapes and ecosystem from Himalayas and high mountain watershed to flood plains of Terai with a low representation in mid mountain area. It is assumed that 80 out of 118 ecosystems of Nepal are covered by the Protected Areas.
Protected Areas mainly consist the National Parks, Wildlife Reserves, Conservation Areas and Hunting Reserves. Despite the efforts of Government of Nepal, different organizations are involved in the preservation of nature and wildlife. Likewise this page helps to increase the awareness among the people about the importance and need of conservation.
